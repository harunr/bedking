A Mattress Buying Guide for Mommies to be

Choosing a mattress with the proper support is the key to a good night’s rest.

Shop Beds Now

Our mattress experts offer 4 points to help you make the best choice

Although stomach sleeping can help prevent heartburn and indigestions as well as reduce snoring and sleep apnea, it really is not the best sleeping position to avoid back and neck pain.

Firmness & Comfort

Walking the line between firmness for support and softness for comfort can be a tall order. Happily, our selection of maternity mattresses combine the best of both. During pregnancy women tend to sleep on their sides, which, in combination with increased weight, can lead to pressure point sensitivity. A mattress with suitable yield prevents soreness by cushioning these sensitive areas.

Cooling Technology

Night sweats are no fun, and your mattress shouldn't make it worse. During pregnancy, the body's core temperature is naturally raised due to increased metabolic demand, which can also lead to uncomfortable temperatures that hamper restful sleep. Our innovative iGel technology ensures that there is plenty of airflow to keep you cool throughout the night.

Weight Distribution

A good maternity mattress facilitates proper weight distribution over its entire surface. This not only provides support for your limbs, belly and spine, but also ensures that both partners enjoy uninterrupted sleep by preventing weight transfer from one end of the bed to the other. This leaves you free to move around, adjust your position and get up with minimal inconvenience to your partner.

Size Matters

Right now you may be choosing the best mattress for pregnancy, but once your baby arrives, there are other matters to consider. Investing in a larger mattress that can accommodate all your support pillows now, will also give you more room for co-sleeping and wiggly toddlers down the line. Not allowing any little people in your bed? No problem - more room to spread out!
