Using Your Phone at Night Without Disrupting Sleep

January 28, 2020 Health + Wellness

Smartphones and tablets are marvellous inventions that have completely changed the way we live.

These days you can do everything from tracking the number of steps you take, log your calories, keep up with far flung family and friends and manage your functions with the click of a few buttons right in the palm of your hand. It’s like magic!

However, as always, great power comes with great responsibility. Smartphone addiction is a real (and scary!) thing. It’s impacting our mental health, has been linked to depression and anxiety, and it has also been proven that it can severely affect our ability to fall and remain asleep at night.

This is why it is so important to manage the time you spend on your phone effectively, so you can enjoy the many benefits of these wonderful devices while minimising the negative effects it can have.

Here are a few ways in which you can adapt your nighttime phone use for improved sleep and general wellbeing:

  • Place It On Night Mode

    Night mode turns down the brightness of your screen and filters out the blue light and most handsets will allow you to turn it on at a given time every day.

  • Customise App Notifications

    Apps like Snapchat and Instagram present trigger notifications to draw you into the app. As a user, you can customise these settings to limit the notifications and use your phone more mindfully.

  • Track Your Usage & Adapt When Necessary

    Many phones have inbuilt functionality to help you track how much time you are spending browsing per day, but there are also apps you can download to help you manage your screen time. This is a helpful way to see when you’re going a little off the rails and may need to revert to other evening diversions for a time.

  • Embrace Sleep Time Apps

    If you battle with insomnia or sometimes struggle to sleep, there are a variety of apps out there that can help you to take control of your nighttime slumber. Whether you choose to meditate, listen to soothing stories or prefer nature sounds, there is bound to be an application that will work for you.
